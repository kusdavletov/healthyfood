How it works?

1
You make an order on the website or on our instagram account
2
You communicate with our consultant
Our employee assists you on the final selection of the program, together you fill in the personal data.
If there is some health related restriction, your information is transmitted to a nutritionist for review and development of a conclusion.
3
We pack your food
After the final choice of the program, the prepared meals are laid out in special sealed containers
that could be reheated in oven or microwave.
4
We deliver your food

On the first day of delivery, payment is made via courier. Delivery is carried out by our courier drivers, who must make a call one hour before departure and deliver food to the address you specify. (Delivery time is from 17:00 to 22:00)

Healthy food delivery is carried out in special thermo-bags. Thermo-bag, ensures the safety of products for 5-6 hours from the moment of delivery. Thermo-bags and cold elements are returnable and transferred to the courier with the next delivery. At the request of the client, a thermo-bag is provided for the entire program period for a deposit value.
